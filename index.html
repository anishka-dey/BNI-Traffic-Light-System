BNI Traffic Lights System

BNI Traffic Lights System consists of 100 points in total. These points are calculated over the period of 6 months. Our goal is to become a Green member. Here are the criterias you need to fulfill.


1] Timing
  • Total points = 5 points
    1. If you join 15 mintues before the meeting starts continuosly for 6 months, you will recieve 5 points
    2. If you are late for just one meeting, for the next 6 months you will recieve 0 points
2] Attendance
  • Total points = 15 points.
    1. If you are present for all the 26 meetings in the span period of 6 months, you will recieve 15 points.
    2. If you are absent for just one meeting in the span period of 6 months, you will recieve 10 points.
    3. If you are absent for two meetings in the span period of 6 months, you will recieve 5 points.
    4. If you are absent for 3 meetings in the span period of 6 months, you will recieve 0 points.
3] Referrals
  • Total points = 20 points
    1. If you provide or get 32 referrals, you will recieve 20 points
    2. If you provide or recieve 26 - 31 referrals, you will recieve 15 points
    3. If you provide or recieve 20 - 25 referrals, you will recieve 10 points
    4. If you provide or recieve 13 - 19 referrals, you will recieve 5 points
    5. If you provide or recieve 0 - 12 referrals, you will recieve 0 points
4] Visitors
  • Total points = 20 points
    1. If you bring 20 visitors, you will recieve 20 points
    2. If you bring 13 - 19 visitors, you will recieve 15 points
    3. If you bring 7 - 12 visitors, you will recieve 10 points
    4. If you bring 3 - 6 visitors, you will recieve 5 points
    5. If you bring 0 - 2 visitors, you will recieve 0 points
5] Thank you Notes
  • Total points = 15 points
    1. If you get a Thank you Note of Rs. 20,00,000 , you will recieve 15 points
    2. If you get a Thank you Note of Rs. 10,00,000 - Rs. 19,99,999 , you will recieve 10 points
    3. If you get a Thank you Note of Rs. 5,00,000 - Rs. 9,99,999 , you will recieve 5 points
    4. If you get a Thank you Note of Rs. 0 - Rs. 4,99,999 , you will recieve 0 points
6] Testimonials
  • Total points = 10 points
    1. If you give 2 testimonials, you will recieve 10 points
    2. If you give 1 testimonial, you will recieve 5 points
    3. If you give 0 testimonial, you will recieve 0 points
7] Trainings
  • Total points = 15 points
    1. If you attend 3 trainings in 6 months, you will recieve 15 points
    2. If you attend 2 trainings in 6 months, you will recieve 10 points
    3. If you attend 1 training in 6 months, you will recieve 5 points
    4. If you attend no trainings in 6 months, you will recieve 0 points
